The club

13th Round is a boxing gym built on fundamentals: footwork you can trust, hands that stay busy, and lungs that last. No ego — just work.

  • Small-group classes
  • Open gym hours
  • Youth & adult

What we train

Boxing fundamentals

Stance, guard, distance, combos, and defence — drilled until they feel second nature.

Conditioning

Bag rounds, skips, sleds when we have floor space — cardio that feels like boxing, not boredom.

Sparring lab

Controlled rounds for intermediate members — coach-led, taped hands, monitored intensity.
